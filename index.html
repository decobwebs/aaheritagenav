Our Journey

AA HERITAGE NAVIGATION LTD

AA HERITAGE NAVIGATION LTD is an integrated maritime, energy logistics, and IT solution-driven provider based in Nigeria. Our structure is explicitly engineered to deliver end-to-end service excellence across the West African region, leveraging technology for operational superiority and compliance.

Our comprehensive service portfolio spans core maritime functions—including ship brokerage, vessel management, and full port operations—and specialized, technology-backed consulting. This unique combination allows us to deploy sophisticated digital solutions, such as the Minimum Safety Standards (MSS) Digital Platform, designed for regulatory enforcement and real-time safety verification of tanker trucks, vessels, and oil & gas facilities.

We are committed to maximizing safety, efficiency, and transparency for clients in both the critical oil & gas and rapidly growing non-oil sectors, positioning AA Heritage as the partner of choice for compliant and high-performance logistics.
